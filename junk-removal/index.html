Frequently asked questions about junk removal

How do you price junk removal?

Pricing is based on volume, weight, and the type of items being removed. We will confirm the scope and provide a clear estimate before starting the job so there are no surprises.

Do I need to move items outside first?

No. Our team does the heavy lifting. Just point out the items you want removed, and we will handle the loading and cleanup.

Can you handle construction debris?

Yes. We can remove renovation debris, drywall, flooring, and leftover materials from remodeling projects. Let us know the details so we can plan the right equipment.

Do you remove appliances and large furniture?

We do. Refrigerators, washers, dryers, couches, and other large items are common removal requests. We will plan for safe handling based on access and weight.

What if I have a full cleanout?

We handle full property cleanouts for garages, basements, and rental units. We will set the right crew size and timeline so the job is finished efficiently.

Do you offer same-week appointments?

We often can, depending on the season and demand. The fastest way to check availability is to send us your details or call directly.

Can you help with estate or downsizing cleanouts?

Yes. We handle estate and downsizing cleanouts with care and efficiency. We can remove furniture, boxes, and leftover items so the home is ready for the next step.

Will you remove yard debris or brush?

Absolutely. We remove yard waste, storm debris, and brush piles. This service pairs well with lawn care and seasonal cleanup work.

Do you provide cleanup after the haul?

Yes. We leave the area tidy and broom-clean so you can use the space right away. Our goal is a finished look, not just an empty space.

Can you remove items from a second floor or basement?

Yes. We handle heavy lifting and can remove items from basements, attics, or upper floors. We will plan for access and any tight stairs or narrow doorways.

Do you offer recurring pickup for businesses?

We can arrange recurring or scheduled pickups for businesses that need ongoing cleanup. Let us know your frequency and we will create a plan that fits your operation.

Can you remove items from storage units?

Yes. We can meet you at a storage unit and handle the full cleanout. If you are closing a unit, we will make sure the space is cleared to your requirements.

Do you handle electronics or e-waste?

We can remove electronics and advise on proper disposal requirements. Let us know what items you have so we can plan the correct handling and recycling approach.

How quickly can you provide a quote?

We can often provide a same-day estimate with photos and a few details. For larger jobs, we may schedule a quick on-site review to ensure accurate pricing.

Do you remove hot tubs or large outdoor items?

We can remove large outdoor items such as hot tubs, sheds, or playsets, depending on access and condition. Share photos so we can plan the right crew and equipment.
