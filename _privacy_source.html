Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 1/22/2022

Welcome to Seacoast Priority Services ("Company", "we", "our", or "us"). Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you visit our website, seacoastpriorityservices.com, or interact with us through any other means.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

Personal Information (provided voluntarily by you):

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Mailing address
  • Service requests or inquiries

Non-Personal Information (collected automatically):

  • IP address
  • Device information

  • Time and date of your visit

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and manage services you request

3. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. However, we may share information with:

  • Trusted subcontractors or partners who perform services on our behalf (under strict confidentiality terms)

We may use cookies and similar tracking technologies to improve user experience and analyze website performance. You can adjust your browser settings to refuse cookies if you prefer.

5. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, misuse, or disclosure. While we follow industry best practices, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

6. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party sites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of those external websites.

7. Your Choices

You may:

  • Opt out of marketing emails by clicking "unsubscribe" at the bottom of our emails
  • Request access, correction, or deletion of your personal information by contacting us

Our services are not directed to individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.
